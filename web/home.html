What is Rabal?

"If you talk to a man in a language he understands, that goes to his head. If you talk to him in his language, that goes to his heart." - Nelson Mandela

The Power of Language is amazing.

Yet sometimes, it becomes an obstacle preventing us from connecting with others.

That's Why Rabal can help.

Rabal is a immersive, live language translator

23 Languages, at your fingertips.

With translations seamlessly streaming to your Google Glass Headset.
